About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Applied Economics at Ibn Zohr University, where I also actively contribute as a Graduate Teaching Assistant. My academic journey is marked by a deep interest in macroeconomics, focusing on macro-finance, monetary policy, and applied macroeconometrics.

My research passion lies in the interplay between macroeconomics and finance, particularly through the exploration of Dynamic Stochastic General Equilibrium (DSGE) models and the impact of financial frictions on economic dynamics.
