About

John Smith is an economist researcher specializing in economic development and growth. He received his Ph.D. in Economics from Harvard University and has published numerous articles on the topic in top economics journals.

John is currently a professor of economics at the University of Chicago, where he teaches graduate and undergraduate courses in macroeconomics and economic development.

Publications

  • Smith, J. (2022). "Economic Development and Growth: A Review of the Literature." Journal of Economic Literature, 60(1), 1-35.
  • Smith, J. and Lee, Y. (2021). "Foreign Aid and Economic Growth: A Panel Data Analysis." Journal of Development Economics, 149, 102590.
  • Smith, J. and Wang, L. (2020). "The Effects of Education on Economic Development: Evidence from China." Journal of Human Capital, 14(2), 227-261.
